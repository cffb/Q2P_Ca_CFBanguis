About us

Planting trees, changing lives
We train farmers in agroforestry and
sustainable land use - so that they can
grow vibrant regional economies, thriving
food systems, and a healthier planet.
Mission
Our mission is to improve
livelihoods of impoverished farmers by revitalizing degraded lands.
Vision
We believe in a world where
farmers leave a legacy of opportunity
through sustainable practices and
productive lands.
